Features

  • Match History
  • Summoner Search
  • Teammate Search

  • Match History

    Easily view your previous games in one glance.

    Match History shows the main information for past games, including duration, time, champion, kda, items, gold, creep score, and wards.

  • Summoner Search

    Want to know how a friend has been playing?

    Search for another summoner from any region.
    Get the match history and look at all the details of how another summoner did recently (even pro players!)
